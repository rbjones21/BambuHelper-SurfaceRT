How to get your token:
  1. Open bambulab.com and log in
  2. Press F12 to open DevTools
  3. Go to Application (Chrome/Edge) or Storage (Firefox) tab
  4. Expand Cookies → bambulab.com
  5. Find and copy the token cookie value
Token valid ~3 months. Your password is NOT stored.
Find at: bambulab.com → account → uid field

About

▼

BambuHelper Surface RT — a port of Keralots/BambuHelper for Microsoft Surface RT running Debian 12 Linux.

Connects to Bambu Lab printers via MQTT over TLS. Supports LAN mode (Developer Mode) and Bambu Cloud mode.
